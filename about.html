About Solvly

Why we exist, in one sentence: everyday problems deserve smart solutions.

"Helping people solve everyday problems with smart, practical products that make life easier."

The idea behind Solvly

Everyone has a list of tiny daily frustrations they've just learned to live with. The phone that vanishes into the car seat gap. The drawer of tangled cables. The suitcase that's full before you finish packing. None of them are big enough to fix — until you realize you fight the same battle every single day.

Solvly exists to end those battles. We hunt down the clever, well-made products that erase a specific everyday problem, test them ourselves, and bring only the winners into one place.

How we choose products

Every product in our catalog has to pass three questions: Does it solve a real problem? Does it work without tools, subscriptions, or a manual? Would we keep using it ourselves? If the answer to any of them is no, it doesn't make the cut — no matter how viral it is.

What you can expect

Honest product pages that lead with the problem, not the specs. Free shipping on orders over $50. A 30-day, no-fine-print return policy, and a 12-month warranty on everything we sell. If a Solvly product doesn't make your day a little easier, we'll make it right.
